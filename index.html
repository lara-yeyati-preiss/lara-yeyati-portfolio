Selected Projects

The Ordinaries

parsons–smithsonian partnership

This project examines the American Revolutionary period by shifting attention from monuments and heroes to the material traces of ordinary life. Drawing from the Smithsonian’s collections, it explores how everyday objects offer a glimpse into how life was built, organized, and imagined.

Role: Concept, data analysis, design, and development

Anatomy of a Horror Hit

parsons–netflix partnership

This project explores horror as a cultural barometer—capturing, distorting, and projecting our collective fears back to us. Using Netflix’s Engagement Report, it examines what the platform’s most-watched horror titles reveal about contemporary anxieties.

Role: Concept, data sourcing and analysis, design, and development
